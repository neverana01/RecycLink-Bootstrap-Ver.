How It Works

RecycLink simplifies the recycling process into three intuitive steps.

List Your Waste

Input the recyclable materials you have (plastic bottles, paper, metal, etc.).

Get Matched

The app automatically connects you with nearby recyclers or junk shops that buy those materials.

Earn & Recycle

Schedule pickup or drop-off, and get paid or earn eco-points.
