Features & How It Works

RecycLink simplifies recycling in 3 easy steps: List Your Waste, Get Matched, Earn & Recycle.

List Your Waste

Input the recyclable materials you have (plastic, paper, metal, etc.).

Get Matched

The app connects you with nearby recyclers or junk shops.

Earn & Recycle

Schedule pickup or drop-off, and get paid or earn eco-points.
