About RecycLink

RecycLink is a digital platform connecting households and businesses to verified recyclers, turning waste into cash and Eco-Rewards while promoting sustainability.

Mission

Empower communities to recycle efficiently and responsibly, creating value from waste while protecting the environment.

Vision

To become the leading platform that inspires sustainable living by making recycling accessible, rewarding, and transparent.

Core Values

  • Sustainability – Advocating environmental care through responsible recycling.
  • Transparency – Ensuring fair pricing and honest transactions across our platform.
  • Innovation – Using technology to simplify and improve the recycling process.
  • Community – Connecting households, junk shops, and recyclers for a shared cause.
  • Integrity – Operating with trust, safety, and accountability in every interaction.

How It Works

RecycLink simplifies the recycling process into three intuitive steps.

List Your Waste

Input the recyclable materials you have (plastic bottles, paper, metal, etc.).

Get Matched

The app automatically connects you with nearby recyclers or junk shops that buy those materials.

Earn & Recycle

Schedule pickup or drop-off, and get paid or earn eco-points.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RecycLink, for both households and recyclers.

For Households / App Users

Download the app, create an account, and list your recyclable items. The system will match you with verified buyers nearby.

Plastics, paper, metals, glass, used electronics, and more. The app automatically categorizes them for you.

You can receive payment through GCash, Maya, or bank transfer—or earn EcoPoints redeemable for vouchers and rewards.

For Recyclers / Junk Shops

Go to “Join as Recycler” on the website or app, submit your business details, and get verified by our team.

Buyers set their own rates, visible to all users. A recommended “market average” guide helps ensure fair trade.

Trust & Safety

Yes. We require valid IDs or business licenses for verification. Verified profiles display Trust Badges for assurance.

We strictly comply with data privacy laws and use data anonymization to safeguard all user information.

Solutions and Support

Contact our support team directly through the app or email support@recyclink.app for quick assistance.

Route tracking, notifications, and verified logistics partners ensure reliable scheduling and quick issue resolution.
